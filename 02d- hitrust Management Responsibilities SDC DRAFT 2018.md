Procedure
1. Human Resources is responsible to assure the workforce properly briefed on their information security roles and responsibilities prior to being granted access to covered information or information systems. This occurs during initial in-person training on the privacy and security programs upon hire, prior to systems access. Security and privacy training content is reviewed yearly by the Security Officer, Privacy Officer, and HR. Additional review occurs by department heads of the MSO and Medical Group. Any mid-year updates to policies may be communicated to HR for training content change by the Security or Privacy Officer. The workforce is additionally made aware of the full suite of privacy and security policies during new employee orientation. They are trained how to access all policies via the Crossover Health intranet. [See Policy 02.e.]
2. The Privacy Officer is responsible for yearly retraining via the compliance management system. [See Policy 02.e.]
3. Testing of awareness and policies occurs monthly as described in the risk assessment policy. [See Policy 03.b.] Oversight and tracking of the testing outcomes is performed by the Privacy Officer and reported to the HIPAA Steering Committee. Any anomalies will be addressed per the Risk Mitigation Policy. [See Policy 03.c.]
