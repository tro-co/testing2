Procedure
1. Prior to hire, Crossover Health Human Resources (HR) engages a third-party service for background checks. The candidate receives a link from the service to create a secure account. Once account is created, the candidate signs a release form. The third-party service conducts comprehensive screening to include education, public record, credit, and professional licenses. The results are returned to Crossover Health HR. If negative findings are present, HR will:
a. Review for discrepancies in what was provided in application.
b. If the finding pertains to clinical professionals, Credentialing will conduct verification.
2. The finding(s) will be reviewed for severity and risk to essential functions of their jobs to determine if candidate may be an acceptable employee.
